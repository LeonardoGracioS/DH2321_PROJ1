What is this?

This visualization was made for a project in DH2321 at KTH. The goal was to form the best groups according to a dataset Student_Skills.csv containing the different skills of the students.

The parallel coordinates displays the skills of the students for different criterias. Then, you can easily build your group with the skills that you want.

Never heard of parallel coordinates? Read this tutorial.

How to use?

One can consider that a good group is a group with complementary skills. According to the skills of a student, brush higher or lower levels of skills for each column.
The remaining students will give the best results for your choice.
You can also export them.

Controls

Brush: Drag vertically along an axis.
Remove Brush: Tap the axis background.
Reorder Axes: Drag a label horizontally.
Invert Axis: Tap an axis label.
Remove Axis: Drag axis label to the left edge.
